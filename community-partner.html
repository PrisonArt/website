Community Partners

Any Prison Community Partner must create a digital wallet to which 20% of token proceeds will be programmatically sent, immediately upon sale.

The Pr1s0n Art Project itself uses a Gnosis Safe wallet, which requires multiple signatures to authorise any transaction and is therefore safer. However, if you have no experience with this technology, we recommend you begin by setting up MetaMask, a browser extension which is easy and will live in the top right of your screen for whenever you need it.

Requirements

Only digital copies are sold through Pr1s0n Art. You will be responsible for gathering the physical pieces to be sold, digitizing them (using high-definition photography), and collecting the information we need about each participating artist to ensure transparency and accountability. Most importantly, we will need you to provide:

  • An inmate release form authorizing you to digitize their art and sell the digital copy. This form must detail the
    • Name
    • Inmate ID
    • Prisoner Jurisdiction
    • Specific electronic payment addresses for each artist: for restitution fees, court fees, etc.
  • Authorization of the incarcerating jurisdiction.
  • Curated biographical/auto-biographical write-ups, video, essays, poetry regarding artists and pieces that create provenance and help promote the work.
  • It'd also be helpful for you to participate in the promotion of the artworks/artists.

Diving Deeper

"This artwork isn't out to make an overtly political statement. Like all good art, it organises elements in the world around it in order to make an audience see and feel in a new way."

We believe we can use public, programmable, transparent flows of value in order to cultivate compassion. We don't wish to establish a new world order: if we make a genuine and felt difference the life of just one person, that will be enough. If we illustrate the kinds of collective economic storytelling practices required to create new systems of restorative justice, then it will be a dream come true.
